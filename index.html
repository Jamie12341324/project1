What is the awakening?

The Vita have reigned over the surface world for too long! The Vita which means latin for life are creatures that only use the same amount of energy to make things as the energy they get from breaking down chemcial bonds in their stomach. So the total amount of chemcial potential energy stays the same. This means that if you add the energy from sunlight that plants harvest then the energy in the system of life goes up over time However other life forms use energy they didn't make which converts chemcial potential energy into thermal energy. This reduces the amount of chemcial potential energy so the total energy left decreases. This is unsustainable. The awakening is a mass release of energy built up by vita over time and vomited up by the non-vita. This energy powers Titans.
